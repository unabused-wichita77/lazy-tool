## Our standard

We want `lazy-tool` to be a technically sharp, respectful open source project.

Participants are expected to contribute in ways that improve the project, the documentation, and the discussion quality around it.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Give technical feedback without personal attacks.
- Critique ideas and implementation choices, not people.
- Prefer evidence, benchmarks, and code over hand-wavy opinion.
- Help keep the repo honest: no overclaiming, no hype-driven nonsense.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or abuse
- Personal insults
- Discrimination
- Doxxing
- Bad-faith spam or trolling
- Repeated low-quality disruption after clear moderation

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.
